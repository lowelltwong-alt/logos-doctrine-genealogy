# Profile Scope Rules

Allowed profile scopes are mirrored in
[../registry/profile_scopes.yaml](../registry/profile_scopes.yaml).

Rules:

- Do not infer a profile scope for real historical material.
- Do not collapse contested doctrine into universal truth.
- Descriptive presence of a tradition or profile is not endorsement.
- Future additions require upstream governance owner approval.
- Graph rank, vector similarity, semantic similarity, and generated confidence
  cannot assign scope.
