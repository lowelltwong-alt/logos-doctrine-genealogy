# Authority Model

This repo's authority level is `interpretive_historical_profile_scoped`.

It can later describe who said what, when, how a formulation developed, and how
traditions or profiles receive or dispute it. Those descriptions must remain
scoped, sourced, reviewed, and provenance-bearing.

This repo does not override:

- `logos-governance-architecture` for policy and vocabulary;
- `logos-scripture-graph` for canonical Scripture data and passage IDs;
- `logos-boundary-literature` for boundary/reception source-corpus ownership.
